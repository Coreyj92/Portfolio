I passed the class with an A but once i moved to Texas in 2011 I had to become an adult alot sooner than I wanted and overtime my love for technology started to fade away. In 2014 I was hired with the USPS to become a mail carrier and that is still my occupation till this day! Even though I love my job, the bonds i build with co-workers and the relationships I built with my customers on and off the clock. I'm just at a point in life where I see the decrease in business with the company also the increase of cost of living and I'm just trying to stay ahead of the curve. Now I have the opportunity to enroll back into school for the first time in over 10 years and to respark my love for technology which at the moment is bringing in a new excitement in my life even though I have days where class is cooking my brain lol.
